1. The Vision

A global eco-infrastructure framework uniting low-cost, lifetime micro-agriculture hardware with a gamified, skill-based barter network. Designed for universal deployment starting at a 5-gallon container scale, our ecosystem removes cash dependencies from local resource management.

2. The Hardware: Three Standalone Agrisystems

We have engineered three distinct, specialized hardware systems that mount universally onto standard 5-gallon containers and up, allowing all ages to grow food without complex configurations:

  • 🌱 System A: The Sprout (Soil & Canopy Ecosystem) – Focused on rain catchment, soil volume, and light tracking. Features a built-in mechanical rainwater diverter and uses a 4-color LED matrix (Red, Orange, Purple, Lime Green) to monitor solar exposure and tell you when to rotate your plant or add soil.
  • 💧 System B: The Harvest (Core Purification Unit) – Our high-volume water purification unit. Combines solar-powered UV-C micro-LEDs and a natural, chemical-free bio-filter stack (Moringa seed paste, pine bark, activated charcoal). Uses a 3-color LED matrix (Blue, Green, Yellow) to signal water needs, nutrient shifts, and harvesting windows.
  • 🍄 System C: The Bloom (Mycelium & Inflorescence System) – Specialized for high-humidity flower blooming and mushroom mycelium growth. Features a specialized low-pressure distribution manifold and uses a 3-color LED matrix (Turquoise, Burgundy, Null) to identify blooming shifts and prevent crop contamination.

3. Skill-Based Barter Platform

A cozy, serious-gaming interface that connects communities without fiat currencies. Users initialize the app with their real-world trade (Electrician, Mechanic, Chef, Handyman) and receive baseline agricultural training. The ecosystem routes local labor exchanges, allowing non-farming trades to barter infrastructure maintenance or manual work directly for agricultural surplus.
